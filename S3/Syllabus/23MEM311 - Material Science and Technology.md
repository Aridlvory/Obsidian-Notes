# MODULE 1 (11 hours)
- [ ] Classification of Engineering Materials
- [ ] Earlier and present development of atomic structure – Earlier and present development in primary bonding
- [ ] Ionic, covalent and Metallic bonding, its properties
- [ ] Secondary bonds – van der walls bonding
- [ ] Crystallography, Unit cells, SC, BCC, FCC and HCP unit cells
- [ ] Calculation of no of atoms per unit cell, Calculations of theoretical density
- [ ] Miller Indices - Calculations
# MODULE II (11 hours)
- [ ] Crystal imperfections, its classification and examples,
- [ ] Solid Solutions, Phase diagrams,
- [ ] Lever rule, simple problems, Iron Iron-carbide phase diagram
- [ ] Development of microstructure in iron-carbon alloys,
- [ ] Invariant Reactions; Phase transformation
- [ ] Isothermal transformation diagram, TTT and CCT diagrams, applications.
# MODULE III (8 hours)
- [ ] Modes of plastic deformation
- [ ] Strengthening mechanisms
- [ ] Types of fracture, brittle and ductile fracture
- [ ] Fatigue, Stress cycles, fatigue tests, S-N Curve
- [ ] Ductile to brittle transition temperature (DBTT) in steels
- [ ] Creep: Creep curves, creep tests
# MODULE IV (10 hours)
- [ ] Composite materials, fiber and matrix phases, its functions
- [ ] Rule of mixtures, density and stiffness calculations,
- [ ] Polymer matrix composites
- [ ] Metal matrix composites and ceramic matrix composites
- [ ] Dielectric materials, conductors, resistor materials
# MODULE V (8 hours)
- [ ] Superconducting phenomenon, properties and applications
- [ ] Semiconductor materials, properties and applications
- [ ] Fabrication of integrated circuits,
- [ ] semiconductors devices

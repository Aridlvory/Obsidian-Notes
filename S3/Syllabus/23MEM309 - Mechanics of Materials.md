# MODULE 1 (10 Hours)

- [ ] Deformation behaviour of elastic solids in equilibrium under the action of a system of forces, method of sections.
- [ ] Stress vectors on Cartesian coordinate planes passing through a point, stress at a point in the form of a matrix.
- [ ] Equality of cross shear, Cauchy's equation.
- [ ] Displacement, Cartesian strain matrix, strain- displacement relations (small-strain only),
- [ ] Simple problems to find strain matrix.
- [ ] Tutorial Session
- [ ] Principal planes and principal stress, meaning of stress invariants, maximum shear stress
- [ ] Mohr’s circle construction
- [ ] Problems for 2D case using Mohr circle

# MODULE II (8 Hours)

- [ ] Stress–Strain curves of Ductile and Brittle Materials, Poisson’s ratio.
- [ ] Constitutive equations-generalized Hooke’s law,
- [ ] Equations for linear elastic isotropic solids in terms of Young’s Modulus and Poisson’s ratio,
- [ ] Hooke’s law for Plane stress and plane strain conditions Relations between elastic constants E, G, ν and K.
- [ ] Calculation of stress and strain in axially loaded members
- [ ] Calculation of deformation in axially loaded members
- [ ] Thermal stress and thermal strain.
- [ ] Tutorial Problems from thermal stresses and strains

# MODULE III (10 Hours)

- [ ] Torsional deformation of circular shafts
- [ ] Assumptions for shafts subjected to torsion within elastic deformation range
- [ ] Torsional rigidity, Polar moment of inertia
- [ ] Basic design of transmission shafts.
- [ ] Simple problems to estimate the stress in solid and hollow shafts.
- [ ] Shear force and bending moment diagrams for cantilever
- [ ] Tutorial problems based on loading of cantilever.
- [ ] Shear force and bending moment diagrams for simply supported beams.
- [ ] Shear force and bending moment diagrams for simply supported beams.

# MODULE IV (10 Hours)

- [ ] Normal and shear stress in beams: section modulus, flexural rigidity
- [ ] Numerical problems to evaluate bending stress
- [ ] Economic sections.
- [ ] Shear stress formula for beams, shear stress distribution for a rectangular section.
- [ ] Tutorial on shear stress distribution
- [ ] Deflection of beams using Macauley’s method.
- [ ] Tutorial problems to find deflection
- [ ] Design of beams for a given loading
- [ ] Elastic strain energy and Complementary strain energy.
- [ ] Elastic strain energy for axial loading, transverse shear, bending and torsional loads.

# MODULE V (10 Hours)

- [ ] Fundamentals of buckling and stability, critical load
- [ ] Equilibrium diagram for buckling of an idealized structure
- [ ] Buckling of columns with pinned ends
- [ ] Euler’s buckling theory for long columns.
- [ ] Critical stress, slenderness ratio, Rankine’s formula for short columns.
- [ ] Tutorial problems on column buckling
- [ ] Stresses due to combined loads (axial, bending)
- [ ] Stresses due to combined loads (torsion)
- [ ] Practical problems involving combined loading - Case study report submission (Group work)

# MODULE 1 [Fluid Properties and Fluid Statics] (10 Hours)
- [ ] Properties of Fluid: Introduction: Fluids and continuum
- [ ] Physical properties of fluids, density, specific weight, vapour pressure
- [ ] Newton’s law of viscosity. Ideal and real fluids, Newtonian and non-Newtonian fluids. Properties of Fluid (simple problems only).
- [ ] Fluid Statics: Pascal’s Law, Hydrostatic law, pressure.
- [ ] Pressure measurement using simple manometers, differential manometers. Simple problems
# MODULE II [Fluid Kinematics and dynamics] (10 Hours)
- [ ] Fluid Kinematics: Introduction, types of flow- steady and unsteady flow, uniform and nonuniform flow, compressible and Incompressible flow, rotational and irrotational flow (definition only).
- [ ] streamline, streak line, path lines and stream tubes. Continuity equation, Simple Problems.
- [ ] Momentum Equation: Euler's equation, Bernoulli’s equation (no derivation), hydraulic coefficients
- [ ] venturi meter, orifcemeter, pitot tube (simple problems). Flow over triangular and rectangular notches (theory only), Pitot tube.
# MODULE III [Flow Through Pipes] (10 Hours)
- [ ] Type of flow –Reynolds experiments, Reynolds number.
- [ ] Laminar flow: Hagen-Poiseuille equation, laminar flow through circular pipes.
- [ ] Turbulent flow Frictional losses in pipe flow-Darcy’s and Chezy’s equation, major and minor losses. Boundary layer concept (Theory only).
- [ ] Darcy’s and Chezy’s equation (simple problems)
- [ ] Major and minor losses in pipe flow-Problems Boundary layer concept (Theory only)
# MODULE IV (10 Hours)
- [ ] Introduction of rotodynamic machines, Classification of turbines – Working principles.
- [ ] Pelton wheel and Francis turbine – Work done, Efficiencies, specific speed.
- [ ] Pelton wheel and Francis turbine – Work done, Efficiencies, specific speed. (Problems) 
# MODULE V (8 Hours)
- [ ] Classification of pumps – Centrifugal pumps, reciprocating pump, deep well, pump, submersible pump, Gear pump, vane pump
- [ ] Working principle. Performance of centrifugal pump-head, efficiency, workdone
- [ ] Characteristics curves of centrifugal pumps. Water hammer, Cavitation, NPSH.
